How NeuroCredit works

01
Data Layer

Submit alternative financial signals

Instead of a bank statement, you provide UPI transaction patterns, bill payment history, income sources, and savings behaviour. 14 signals, no traditional credit history required.

02
AI / ML Layer

XGBoost model computes your score

Our gradient-boosted model — trained on 5,000 synthetic profiles mirroring India's informal economy — processes your signals and outputs a score from 300 to 900 in under a second.

03
Blockchain Layer

Score is minted as a Soul Bound Token

Your credit score is written to a non-transferable ERC-5484 NFT (SBT) on Polygon. The token is bound permanently to your wallet — it cannot be sold, gifted, or faked. Only the issuer can update it.

04
Privacy Layer

Lenders verify without seeing your data

A lender calls the smart contract's verifyCredit(wallet) function. They get back: score, grade, eligibility. Nothing else. Your raw data is never on-chain.

05
Credit Access

Access credit products you were locked out of

Partner lenders and DeFi protocols can instantly assess your creditworthiness. No paperwork, no branch visit, no discrimination against gig workers or the self-employed.
